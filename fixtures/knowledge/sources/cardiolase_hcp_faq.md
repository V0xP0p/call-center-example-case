# Cardiolase HCP Frequently Asked Questions

## Approved Use Context

Cardiolase is a mock product used in this example corpus for cardiovascular support scenarios. This document is intended for healthcare professionals only.

## Commonly Reported Adverse Reactions

Observed adverse reactions in approved materials include dizziness, nausea, vomiting, fatigue, and headache. HCPs should evaluate patients based on clinical judgment and local standards of care.

## Reporting Safety Events

If a healthcare professional becomes aware of a serious or unexpected adverse event, the event should be reported promptly through the company's safety intake channel. The initial report should include an identifiable reporter, an identifiable patient, a suspect product, and an event description.

## Medical Information Boundary

This FAQ does not provide patient-specific treatment recommendations. For urgent patient care decisions, providers should rely on clinical judgment and emergency procedures as appropriate.
