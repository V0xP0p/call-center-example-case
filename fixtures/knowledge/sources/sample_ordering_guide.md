# Cardiolase Sample Ordering Guide

## Eligibility

Verified healthcare professionals may request product samples only after identity verification against the approved local HCP roster.

## Ordering Steps

1. Confirm the healthcare professional identity and practice record.
2. Check the doctor's current sample availability and quantity limits.
3. Tell the doctor which sample products are available to order.
4. Confirm the selected product and requested quantity.
5. Create the sample-order form and confirm submission details.

## Constraints

- Orders must stay within the doctor's currently available quantity limit.
- Agents must not offer samples that are not listed in the doctor's available inventory.
- Agents should keep the response procedural and avoid unsupported product claims.
