ADVERSE EVENT INTAKE SOP - REVISION 4

Section 1  Purpose
This procedure describes the minimum information needed to create a reportable safety case.

Section 2  Minimum information
1) identifiable reporter
2) identifiable patient
3) suspect product
4) adverse event description

Section 3  Seriousness indicators
hospitalization
life-threatening event
death
disability or incapacity
other medically significant event

Section 4  Agent instructions
Agents must not provide diagnosis or treatment direction.
Agents should collect minimum information and escalate according to safety policy.
